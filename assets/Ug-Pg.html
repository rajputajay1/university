Some of our Top Programs:

--------------- ---------------

Master of Business Administration (MBA) – This is a postgraduate course for aspirants who want to make a career in the field of business. The course focuses on equipping students with knowledge and skills required for managing an organization. It provides them with an understanding of different industries, their operations, clientele and market trends.

Human Resource (HR) – This course helps students to understand the HR function of an organization and its role in human resource management. It helps them develop skills required for effective recruitment, selection and training of employees.

B.Com / M. Com –This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in commerce. It provides students with an understanding of accounting and finance, management and marketing. The course also provides students with skills required for effective decision-making in today’s business environment.

BA. English / MA. English – This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in English. It provides students with skills required for effective communication and analysis of literature, language and culture.

BA. Psychology / MA. Psychology – This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in psychology. It provides students with skills required for effective understanding and treatment of psychological disorders.

BA. History / MA. History– This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in history. It provides students with skills required for effective research, critical analysis and presentation of historical facts.

BA. Philosophy / MA. Philosophy – This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in philosophy. It provides students with skills required for effective understanding and analysis of philosophical concepts.

BA. Economics / MA. Economics –This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in economics. It provides students with skills required for effective economic analysis, forecasting and policymaking.

BA. English Literature / MA. English Literature – Linguistics – This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in English literature. It provides students with skills required for effective understanding and analysis of literary concepts.

BA. Political Science / MA. Political Science - This course is an undergraduate/ postgraduate program in political science which provides students with skills required for effective analysis and evaluation of political processes, institutions and policies.
